Coding Components & Feasibility Report

Ant Design

One of the biggest reasons I chose to cover financial literacy in the first place is the lack of accessible and updated UI surrouding the topic on the web. I believe that ant design will provide me with common design patterns and popular UI that can bridge the gap between financial literacy and the younger generation.

Chart.js

For the same reason I am choosing to use ant design instead of next.js, I am using chart.js instead of D3. I intentionally want the UI to be very straight forward.
